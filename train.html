About Us

We are a group of retired experienced athletes who because of past physical health problem were forced to make a change towards living a healthier lifestyle. We all faced similar experiences and our journey we all became some of the fittest and top compeditors in a variety of world sports competitions. Now our mission to help everyday people who wants to make a change to have the tools and knowledge they need to acheive a good healtyh lifestyle.

Together we have created Good Life gym to do just that. With all the experiences of diets, training and life style changes we experienced. Our specially trained personnels at Good Life will assit you in every way possible and guide you in the right direction to acheive great health and sustainability

We have also create a facility which contain specially tested equipments that were specially designed to give more gains with less effort. Our staff are also trained to provide you with "How To Use" demonstrations so you never feel like you do not know what you are doing or use the equipments in the wrong way
